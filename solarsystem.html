The Solar System

Overview of the solar system

Our solar system consists of the Sun and everything bound to it by gravity, including planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and meteoroids.
Information on planets:
  1. Mercury:
    The smallest and closest to the Sun.
  2. Venus:
    Earth’s "sister planet."
  3. Earth:
    The only planet known to support life.
  4. Mars:
    The "Red Planet" and a key target for exploration.
  5. Jupiter:
    The largest planet with 79 moons.
  6. Saturn:
    Famous for its rings.
  7. Uranus:
    An ice giant.
  8. Neptune:
    Known for its deep blue color.
