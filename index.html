Intro

Space exploration is the investigation of outer space through manned and unmanned spacecraft. It has fueled humanity’s curiosity about the universe and expanded our knowledge of planets, stars, and galaxies.

Milestones in space exploration:

  • The first satellite: Sputnik 1 (1957)
  • The first human in space: Yuri Gagarin (1961)
  • The Moon landing: Apollo 11 (1969)
