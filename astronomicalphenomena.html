Astronomical Phenomena

The universe is filled with awe-inspiring phenomena that continue to fascinate astronomers and stargazers alike.

Some of the most intriguing include:

  • Black Holes:

    • Regions of space with gravitational forces so strong that not even light can escape.

  • Supernovae:

    • Massive explosions marking the death of a star.

  • Nebulae:

    • Giant clouds of gas and dust, often where new stars are born.

  • Eclipses:

    • When one celestial body moves into the shadow of another, like solar and lunar eclipses.

  • The Big Bang:

    • The theory explaining the origin of the universe around 13.8 billion years ago.
